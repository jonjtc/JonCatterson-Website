Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you close?

I can close in as little as 7-14 days, depending on your needs.

Do I need to make repairs?

No, I buy homes as-is, saving you time and money.

Is there any obligation?

Not at all—just a no-pressure conversation to explore your options.

What types of houses do you buy?

I buy all types of houses, regardless of condition, age, or location in the Sacramento area. Whether it's a fixer-upper, inherited property, or a home that's in great shape but you need to sell quickly, I'm interested.

How do you determine the offer price?

I base my cash offer on the home's current "as-is" value, recent comparable sales in the area, and any necessary repairs or updates. My goal is to provide a fair, competitive price that works for both of us without the need for appraisals or negotiations.

Are there any fees or commissions?

No, there are no fees, commissions, or hidden costs when selling to me. Unlike traditional real estate agents who charge 5-6% in commissions, I cover all closing costs, making the process simple and cost-free for you.

What if my house is in foreclosure?

I can help if your home is facing foreclosure. With a quick cash offer, we can close before the foreclosure process completes, potentially saving your credit and giving you peace of mind.

Can I sell if I have tenants in the property?

Yes, I buy properties with tenants. I can handle the details, whether it's assuming the lease or working out a smooth transition, so you don't have to worry about evictions or tenant issues.

How is selling to you different from listing with an agent?

Selling to me is faster and hassle-free—no need for open houses, staging, or waiting for buyers. You get a cash offer in days, close on your schedule, and avoid agent commissions or repair demands.

What areas do you serve?

I primarily serve Sacramento County and surrounding areas in California. If your property is nearby, reach out—I’m flexible.
